Privacy policy
==============

1. Collection and use of personal information

This site does not store a lot of personal information
about you, but it does require you to provide a valid
email address. This will be used for the purpose of
administering your account. It will not be displayed
on the site, used for marketing purposes, or provided
to any third party.

2. Updating your information

You may update your email address or change your
password at any time by logging into the site.

3. Account termination

Upon deletion of your account, your email address will
be purged from the system.
